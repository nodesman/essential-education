# Story: Defending Your Reality

**Goal:** To provide the reader with actionable strategies to recognize, resist, and defend against attacks on their perception of reality.

**Target Book:** `books/narrative_control/`
**Target File:** `defending_your_reality.md`

## Acceptance Criteria

- [ ] The chapter opens by framing the defense of one's reality as a critical act of self-preservation.
- [ ] It provides techniques for grounding oneself in objective facts (e.g., journaling, reality checks with trusted friends).
- [ ] It offers clear scripts and methods for disengaging from bad-faith arguments that seek to distort reality.
- [ ] It empowers the reader to see their "sanity" not as something to be questioned, but as something to be actively defended.
